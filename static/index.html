    • Welcome to GradebookTT!

    GradebookTT is a web application designed to assist students with keeping track of their grades for their University. Registered users are free to add semesters, add courses to semesters, and add marks to courses. This information is then organized and presented in a user-friendly format via the "Grades" tab.

    school

    Profile

    The 'Profile' tab enables logged in users to change their password and university. The university determines the marking scale that is used for grades and GPAs.

    dashboard

    Dashboard

    The 'Dashboard' tab enables logged in users to add/modify their semesters, courses, and marks to reflect the current state of their academics.

    grading

    Grades

    The 'Grades' tab enables logged in users to view detailed summaries for their enrolled semesters, and includes courses, coursework marks, grades, and GPAs.
